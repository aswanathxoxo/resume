Imperial Attire

About Imperial Attire

Imperial Attire is an e-commerce website for a clothing brand, created as our team project. The goal was to develop a user-friendly and secure online shopping platform.

Features

User Account Management

Users can create accounts, log in securely, and manage their profiles and purchases.

Product Catalog and Shopping Cart

Users can browse products, add them to their cart, and proceed to checkout.

Admin Dashboard

Admins can manage the product catalog, adding, updating, and removing items.

Technologies Used

  • Spring Boot
  • Maven
  • JSP (JavaServer Pages)
  • MySQL
